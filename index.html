About Me

Welcome, y’all! My name is Tracie and I’m a Ragin’ Cajun from
South Louisiana who’s ready to embark on a new career journey.

In my previous life, I dedicated several years working
in the medical field. While I deeply valued the work I did there,
I stumbled upon a newfound passion for technology and innovation.
So, here I am in my first semester majoring in Software Engineering,
ready to explore the thrilling adventure of algorithms and data structures.

My aspiration is to become a Game Developer, and I’m fully aware that
to achieve that goal, I must start from the very bottom.
It's like leveling up in a game,
where you need to master the basics before
tackling the epic quests.

Let’s discover new horizons and create digital wonders,
turning this passion into reality!
